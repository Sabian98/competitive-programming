B - Hop Out
Ranklist
Time Limit: 2 second(s) Memory Limit: 32 MB

Your friend Intela threw a challenge to you and you have accepted it. According to the challenge your task was “Starting from the centre of your university's circular play ground you have to come out of the ground by hopping (Crossing any point on the circumference will be counted as coming out of the circle)”. You begin to hop in a straight line from the center to the circumference. Your first hop is R/2 feet , where R is the radius of the circular ground. You continue to hop each time half the length of your previous hop. Now I am throwing a challenge to you. You have to determine whether it is possible to come out of the ground of radius R within N hops or not.

Input

Input starts with an integer T (T <= 50) , denoting the number of test cases.Each case contains a line with 2 integers R, N (0<R<100000 and 0<N<1000) .

Output

For each case , print the case number and print “POSSIBLE” if it is possible to come out within N hops or print “IMPOSSIBLE” if the number of hops is not sufficient.

Sample Input Sample Output
1
10 1
 Case #1 : IMPOSSIBLE
